Excision Presents

location: Legend Valley, Ohio

Dates: September 27th - September 29th

The Experience

Lost Lands is the gateway to an immersive ecosystem of prehistoric wonder. An earth-shattering spectacle of immense music, colossal sound, and mind-bending production.

This year, we’re diving even deeper into the ambiance with more environmental embellishments, mesmerizing decor, bigger volcanoes, better production, and 3x more life-sized dinosaurs, including some you can ride.

Lost Lands gives the world’s greatest Bass Music artists a platform to showcase their skills at full force. One million watts of bass will be blasting PK Sound’s state-of-the-art rigs between two full-size main stages which will themselves be even grander than before.

After the headliners have closed out the main stages, the Sound Camps will already be bumping so you can keep the energy alive in the campgrounds. The Sound Camps are brought to you by your fellow Headbangers, whose lineups include a mix of headliners and talented locals. The phase one lineup for Sound Camps just dropped. Check it out below!
