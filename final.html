The Top Music Festivals to Attend

I enjoy attending music festivals and wish others would experience the amazing atmosphere of music festivals. I know that most of these music festivals can be very expensive to attend so I wanted to create a website that showed a little bit about each music festival that I believe to be some of the best and tell a little bit about what each one represents.

The music festivals that I have mentioned are all EDM based festivals. EDM stands for Electric Dance Music. EDM is not just one style of music, you can have dubstep, house, heavy bass, trance, and lots of other types of EDM.
